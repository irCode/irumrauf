Irum Rauf

Research Fellow, The Open University

I am Research Fellow at The Open University, U.K. I am a member of the Software Engineering And Design(SEAD) Lab and Digital Health Lab . My current research interests lie at the intersection of software engineering, software security and human experiences with technology.

I have worked in multi-disciplinary, multi-generational and multi-national envioronments. I believe that software and other technologies do not exist in isolation - its the way humans use them and how they are developed with the integration of knowledge from various disciplines that bring value to the end-product.
